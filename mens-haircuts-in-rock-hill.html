Frequently Asked Questions About Men's Haircuts

What types of men's haircuts do you specialize in at Carolina Barber Co?
We specialize in classic and modern men's cuts including fades (high, mid, and low), beard trims, straight razor shaves, and traditional scissor cuts. Our barbers are experienced with all hair types and can handle everything from a simple business cut to more detailed styles like pompadours, undercuts, and textured crops. We also offer hot towel treatments and eyebrow trimming to complete your grooming experience.
How much do haircuts cost and do you accept walk-ins?
Our standard men's haircuts start at $25, with beard trims at $15 and full service cut and beard packages at $35. We do accept walk-ins, but we recommend calling ahead or booking online since we can get busy, especially on weekends and after work hours. Wait times for walk-ins are typically 15-30 minutes during weekdays, but can be longer on Saturdays. Appointments guarantee your preferred time slot.
Are your barbers licensed and how long have they been cutting hair?
Yes, all our barbers are fully licensed by the South Carolina Board of Cosmetology and maintain current certifications. Our team has a combined 40+ years of experience, with each barber having at least 5 years in the industry. We regularly attend training workshops to stay current with the latest techniques and trends. You can feel confident that you're getting a professional cut from skilled, certified barbers.
How long does a typical haircut take and what's included in the service?
A standard haircut typically takes 30-45 minutes, depending on the complexity of the style and whether you're adding services like a beard trim. Every cut includes a consultation to discuss what you want, the haircut itself, styling, and a neck cleanup with a straight razor. We also include a hot towel finish and light scalp massage. If you're getting a beard trim as well, plan for about an hour total.
What if I'm not satisfied with my haircut? Do you offer any guarantees?
Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not happy with your cut, please let us know within 48 hours and we'll make it right at no charge. We'll either adjust the cut to better match what you wanted or have another barber take a look if needed. We've been serving the Rock Hill community for years and our reputation depends on making sure every customer leaves happy. Most issues can be resolved with minor adjustments during your visit, so don't hesitate to speak up if something doesn't look right.
